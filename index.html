About

Paul addressed his letter to the Romans "To all in Rome who are loved by God and called to be His holy people." We are loved by God and called to be His holy people here in Waterloo Region. LovedAndCalled.ca exist to celebrate what God is doing through His people and connect us 168 hours a week.

LovedAndCalled.ca is managed by a team that wants to see all believers in the Region in complete unity as Jesus prayed in John 17.
